Privacy Policy
Data Collection

We collect personal information that you provide to us when applying for scholarships, including but not limited to your name, contact information, educational background, and financial information.

Data Usage

Your information is used solely for the purpose of processing your scholarship application, determining eligibility, and communicating with you about your application status.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with scholarship committee members and educational institutions solely for application processing purposes.
